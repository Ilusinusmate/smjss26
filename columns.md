## Question Types

- **Single Choice**: Radio button selection (one answer)
- **Multiple Choice**: Checkbox selection (multiple answers possible)
- **Likert Scale**: 5-point agreement or frequency scale
- **Open-Ended**: Free text responses
- **Matrix**: Multiple sub-questions with same scale
